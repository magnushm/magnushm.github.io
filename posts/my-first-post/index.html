Lundahl SR501 Output Module

October 7, 2021 One-minute read

I put together an output module for my eurorack synth with some old Lundahl SR501 transformers from the late 60’s I had in my box of electronics scrap. It sounds pretty good and doubles as a distortion circuit if I overdrive it a bit. A tip for cheap DIY panels is using metersticks/yardsticks/tommestokk, cutting them to the right length and sanding them down, you end up with the measurements pretty much already on the back.
